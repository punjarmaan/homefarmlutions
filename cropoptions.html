Kale

Health Benefits:

1. Kale contains vitamin A, which is important for eye and bone health.
2. Kale contains vitamin C, which aids in cold and chronic disease prevention.
3. Kale contains vitamin K, which is beneficial for blood clotting and bone building.

Growing Tips:

1. Space your kale 18-24 inches apart.
2. Plant your kale in an area with full sun.
3. Plant kale in well-drained, fertile soil that has a pH of 6.5 - 6.8.

Zucchini

Health Benefits:

1. Zucchini is high in antioxidants.
2. Zucchini reduces blood sugar levels.
3. Zucchini contributes to a healthy digestion.

Growing Tips:

1. Use natural or organic pesticide to grow Zucchini.
2. Water Zucchini generously.
3. Compost and mulch your soil.

Eggplant

Health Benefits:

1. Eggplants are high in antioxidants.
2. Eggplants may reduce cholestrol.
3. Eggplants may promote blood sugar control.

Growing Tips:

1. Space eggplant 24 to 36 inches apart.
2. Soaker hoses are a great option for growing eggplant.
3. The more frequently the fruit sets are harvested, the greater the fruit set.
